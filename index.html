Air Signs: Gemini, Libra, and Aquarius

There are three air signs - Gemini (May 22-June 21), Libra (September 22-October 23), and Aquarius (January 20-February 18). Gemini is ruled by Mercury, the planet of communicaation and technology. Libra is ruled by Venus, the planet of love and beauty, and Aquarius is ruled by Uranus, the planet of innovation and non-traditional thinking. If you were born during one of these date-spans, you have an air sun sign! Welcome to the club. If you are an air sign, it's likely that you make friends easily and are very much 'go with the flow'. Ever heard the phrase 'any way the wind blows'? That's about air signs.

Earth Signs: Capricorn, Virgo, and Taurus

There are three Earth signs - Capricorn (December 21-January 19), Virgo (August 22-September 23), and Taurus (April 20-May 21). Capricorn is ruled by sky daddy Saturn, the planet of hard work and life lessons. Virgo is ruled by Mercury, the planet of communication and technology, and Taurus is ruled by Venus, the planet of love and beauty. If you were born during one of these date-spans, you have an earth sun sign! If you are an Earth sign, it's likely that you tend to be hard working, detail oriented, and very loyal, albeit a little stubborn.

Water Signs: Cancer, Scorpio, & Pisces

There are three Water signs - Cancer (June 21-July 22), Scorpio (October 20-November 21), and Pisces (February 18-March 20). Cancer is ruled by the Moon, the 'planet' of emotions and the soul. Scorpio is ruled by Pluto, the planet of the mystical and the underworld, and Pisces is ruled by Neptune, the planet of dreams, artistry, and the collective consciousness. If you were born during one of these date-spans, you have a water sun sign! If you are a water sign, you are likely very in touch with your emotions (even if you avoid feeling them).

Fire Signs: Aries, Leo, & Sagittarius

There are three Fire signs - Aries (March 20-April 20), Leo (July 22-August 23), and Sagittarius (November 22-December 20). Aries is ruled by Mars, the planet of strength, power, and ambition. Leo is ruled by the Sun, the 'planet' of the self - like the sun, your 'life-force', and Sagittarius is ruled by Juipiter, the planet of growth, prosperity, and miracles. If you were born during one of these date-spans, you have a fire sun sign! If you are a fire sign, it's likely that you are passionate about the things you care about, get a little heated in arguments, and are fierce advocates.
